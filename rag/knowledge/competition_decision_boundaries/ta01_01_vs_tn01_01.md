# Decision

Choose `TA01_01` when one source repeatedly attempts authentication against the same target service in a short window, especially with many failures, short sessions, or account/password attempt evidence.

Choose `TN01_01` for a few normal logins, isolated retries, or ordinary business access without sustained failure evidence. Reset-heavy traffic without authentication context is not brute force.
