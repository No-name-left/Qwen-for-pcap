# Decision

Choose `TA01_02` for explicit attempted compromise: exploit payload/signature, abnormal triggering URI, command or SQL injection, directory traversal, webshell upload, CVE exploit action, or matching abnormal response behavior.

Choose `TN01_01` for ordinary HTTP/TLS/DNS and business requests without exploit evidence. Missing payload visibility is uncertainty—not proof of either attack or normality—so use available behavior and aggregates.
