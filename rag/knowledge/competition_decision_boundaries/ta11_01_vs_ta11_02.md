# Decision

Choose `TA11_01` when the attacker/operator actively reaches an existing backdoor, uses a webshell/control endpoint, or drives an interactive shell/control session. The access direction and existing-backdoor context matter.

Choose `TA11_02` when the compromised host initiates outbound callback/C2, often as recurring check-ins, fixed-remote heartbeats, RAT/bot traffic, or small repeated connections. A generic “backdoor” word alone does not determine the phase.
