# Decision

Choose `TA11_02` when multiple behavioral signals support callback: victim-initiated traffic, stable remote endpoint, repeated or periodic small exchanges, abnormal SNI/DNS/URI, or RAT/bot/C2 evidence.

Choose `TN01_01` for normal browsing, updates, DNS and business TLS without malicious repetition/control evidence. Encryption and periodicity alone are not enough; absence of plaintext also does not make traffic normal.
