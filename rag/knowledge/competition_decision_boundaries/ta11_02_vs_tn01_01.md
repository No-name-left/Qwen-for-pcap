# Decision

Choose `TA11_02` when multiple behavioral signals support callback: victim-initiated traffic to a fixed external endpoint, long-running repeated contact, stable multi-second intervals, small or consistent packet sizes, abnormal domain/SNI/URI, unusual port, repeated DNS/SNI, or RAT/bot/C2 evidence.

Choose `TN01_01` for normal browsing, updates, DNS refresh, NTP, cloud sync, telemetry, health checks, WPAD, browser background connections, and business TLS when normal domain/SNI, common service, common port, or business access pattern explains the periodicity. Encryption and periodicity alone are not enough; absence of plaintext also does not make traffic normal.

# Trigger cues

Retrieve this boundary for `beacon_score`, `regularity_score`, `interval_summary`, `fixed_remote_endpoint`, repeated source-initiated endpoint, TLS SNI/DNS repetition, small repeated payload, `benign_periodic_hints`, common ports 53/80/123/443, update/telemetry/WPAD/sync text, or packet-size patterns.
