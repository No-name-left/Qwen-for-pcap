# Decision

Choose `TA43_01` for port or host discovery: many ports/targets, short or failed connections, SYN fanout, little payload, and no service-specific probe. Ordinary multi-port scanning stays `TA43_01`.

Choose `TA43_02` only with vulnerability-discovery evidence: Nikto/OpenVAS/Nessus/Nmap NSE, CVE or plugin probes, unusual HTTP scanner paths, version/banner fingerprinting, or repeated service-specific checks. A service name or port fanout alone is insufficient.
